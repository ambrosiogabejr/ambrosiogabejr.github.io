New Landscape

"Progress is impossible without change, and those who cannot change their minds cannot change anything." -George Bernard Shaw

Define a Culture of Excellence

Team Nashville/Bowling Green will view sales goals as the floor not the ceiling. Customer service will become a premium, all facets of our infrastructure will be focused on the One Nashville/Bowling Green Goal.

New and Recurring Revenue is the key, Nashville/Bowling Green Sales will work together with Marketing, Interactive, Campaign Management, Research and Business Intelligence/Finance and hold each other to a common 2017 revenue goal.

2017 will be a year of change. All of our Business Partners verticals will be dramatically changed. My job is to keep Local Sales aware of the changes and leading them to desire and embrace the change. This will take lots of clear and empowering communication to navigate the year.

Awareness — Communicate clearly and positively to build up desire.

Desire — Encourage sales, get the staff's buy-in.

Knowledge — Continue to train our address on our products, sales basics, the media landscape, and customer service.

Ability — Hold our AEs accountable to what they learn.

Reinforcement — Define our simplified unified sales model through encouragement and empowering feedback.

My role as Director of Local Sales to Area General Manager Brian Marsh

Clearly communicate goals set forth by Brian Marsh to the LSMs and local team

Succinctly communicate all outside market conditions and intelligence to Brian Marsh

Communicate all metrics in the Nashville and Bowling Green markets

Constant communication with my LSMs on local business drivers, looking ahead of trends rather than reacting

Always communicate major changes in local billings with succinct details

Monthly communication between other local market DOSs on One Tennessee initiatives

My goals as DOS are to build Awareness, increase Desire, instill knowledge through training, allow employees to demonstrate their ability, reinforcing and recognizing employees for their adaptation to change and continued revenue generation

Communicate and help the local team navigate through upcoming change

Getting adoption by all employees into upcoming changes, belief in the leadership of the team and our products and ideas

Get Holistic...Be Accountable

Promote a culture of teamwork, inclusiveness, fairness and excellence; continue to address concerns from recent Credo Speak results

Open an active communication on strategies across Tennessee markets on shared accounts

Own Your Zone

Drive New and Recurring Revenue through increased Zone Ownership

We must create a culture of zone focused revenue, this will take a collaborative effort with Research/BI/Marketing/Sales and Interactive to come up with a clean, concise story. What does this do? It gets AE buy-in. General Market Sales will be integrated into a new local/zone focus culture. Buy-in into the zone strategy will create many more new business opportunities.

AEs will own their zone — AEs need to be aware and be accountable to thead goals to reach our zone revenue (It's important to maintain a profit for our partnering MSO's).

Marketing and Research will be key participants on "selling" our AEs on their "zone home". This will be an ongoing communication of owning your zone successes.

We will need to create a zone revenue target with AE ownership. Accountability reports will be set up by BI based on zone history billing. Then we will come up with zone metrics to be met by the AEs in their zone home.

More new business = More volume = number of zone clients = more new revenue. Our goal is to lift AEs to 25 to 30 accounts per month on air.

Increase AE bandwidth by having rip and run VBSS schedules based on zone budgets created by BI and Sales.

Strategic Account Plan

Increase Revenue through Strategic Account Planning

DOS and Client Solution Specialist will be key players in the Strategic Account Planning

DOS along with the LSMs and AEs will identify Strategic Accounts through history reports, kantar and other research tools

LSMs and AEs will go together to conduct a CNA with a Strategic Account

Follow up will be a think tank session with LSM, Planner, Client Solution Specialist, and pertinent Business Partners

Think Tank will include talk of CNA, Share, client objections, the goal is to have resolutions to clients objections or concerns

Use our vast array of tools — music creative, in stream video, On-Demand and attribution studies data

Format a team-oriented presentation

Lunch including all business partners preferably at Comcast Spotlight office

End the meeting with a close! Ask for the order

If a close isn't made, set a date for a final meeting with the client. All partners will get together, LSM, AE, Client Solution Specialist and Business Partners will meet to put a final presentation together to overcome client objections.

BluWave Video Strategy

Drive New and Recurring Revenue through BluWave Productions

The production is delayed two weeks, two months, Comcast Spotlight loses monthly revenue. How much revenue can we save if we manage the process better?

No orders placed in BluWave unless there is a signed contract

All BluWave orders need an Air Date attached

Does the AEs client have a commercial that can run until the new one is completed?

Find a strategy to hold BluWave and the AE accountable to a deadline. We need an escalating plan of communication through a chain of command.

Producer doesn't contact the client within three days of the order input — result is producer allows AE to contact the client

All scripts must be completed with a shoot date within a week of client contact by BluWave, if not AE escalates to LSM

All edits must be completed within a week of the shoot, if not AE escalates to LSM

Once resolution between LSM and BluWave manager has been made, commercial must be completed within 24 hours

If issues still occur, LSM escalates to DOS
